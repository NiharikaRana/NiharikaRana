John Wick

Chapter 3

REALISING ON - 5 AUGUST 2023


"John Wick: Chapter 3 - Parabellum" is the third installment in
the action-packed "John Wick" film series, released in 2019.


The film features intense and expertly choreographed action
sequences, showcasing John Wick's exceptional combat skills,
gun-fu expertise, and resourcefulness. It explores the complex
world of assassins and the consequences of their actions within
the structured society of killers.
